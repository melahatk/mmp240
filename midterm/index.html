The Office

2005, TV-14, 9 Seasons

The Office is an American mockumentary sitcom television series that follows the daily work lives of office employees of the fictional Dunder Mifflin Paper Company branch in Scranton, Pennsylvania. It aired on NBC for a total of 9 seasons, from March 24, 2005 to May 16, 2013.
